Upgrading from a previous iPhone?

Simply put your old iPhone next to your new one, and with a few taps, you can transfer your data automatically. If you need it, you can even get temporary iCloud storage for free to hold everything during the transfer.

Switching from an Android phone?

When you buy a new iPhone, there’s a walk‑through to get you started, and the Move to iOS app makes it easy to transfer your photos, contacts, and more.
